Craft Miner

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four ores in this cave, click on one to get a specific amount of points added to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score to the random number, you lose if your score goes above the random number.

The value of each ore is hidden from you until you mine it.

Each time you win the game, the value of the ores will change.
